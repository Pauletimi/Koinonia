How Koinonia Works

1. Create Your Account

Join our community by creating a secure and verified account. This ensures everyone on the platform is a genuine participant.

2. Provide Help (PH)

Once you are ready, you can make a pledge to provide help to another member of the community. Your account becomes active in the sharing cycle.

3. Get Matched

Our secure system matches you with a community member who is eligible to receive help. All transactions are peer-to-peer.

4. Get Help (GH)

After successfully providing help, you become eligible to receive help from the community, based on the platform's principles of mutual support.
