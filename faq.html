Frequently Asked Questions

Is Koinonia an investment platform?

No, Koinonia is not an investment platform, a bank, or a business. It is a community of people who agree to help each other directly. There are no guarantees, only the shared trust and fellowship of our members.

How is my information kept secure?

We take security and privacy very seriously. All data is protected with end-to-end encryption, and our platform is built on secure infrastructure. Please read our Privacy Policy for more details.

What happens if someone doesn't provide the help they pledged?

Participants who fail to honor their pledges are immediately and permanently removed from the community to protect the integrity of the platform. Our support team is also available to mediate any disputes.
